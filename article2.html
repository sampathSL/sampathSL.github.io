reference : https://www.liferay.com/downloads/liferay-portal/available-releases , http://sourceforge.net/projects/lportal/files/Liferay Portal/6.2.4 GA5/liferay-plugins-sdk-6.2-ce-ga5-20151118111117117.zip/download
